Background

Physician turned
AI Builder

I started my career as a practicing physician in India, managing 600+ COVID-19 patients before pivoting to population-level evidence. That clinical foundation shapes everything I build: I understand the workflows, the endpoints, and the constraints that frontline staff actually face.

For the past five years I've been embedded in federally funded public health programs (HRSA, NIH, CDC, SC DHEC) during graduate school, doing the work that sits between data systems and the people who use them: building dashboards, training non-technical teams, designing reporting pipelines, and shipping tools that make programs run better.

More recently, I've been building AI-powered tools and agents for the same kinds of organizations: a Claude-powered voice intake system for community health caseworkers, an AI research study design platform, and an intelligent model router that makes LLM access affordable for resource-constrained teams.
